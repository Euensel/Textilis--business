Who We Are

Textilis is a textile manufacturing company based in the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), specializing in the production of high-quality fabrics. We offer a wide range of textiles, including neutral fashion fabrics for all styles, traditional African wax prints, and fully customized fabrics for fashion creators.

Our Services

  • Textiles: A variety of fashion fabrics, including African wax prints and locally/internationally sourced materials.
  • Custom Fabrics: Personalized design services for designers and brands to create unique collections.
  • Apparel Line (Future Expansion): Plans to launch a ready-to-wear collection using our fabrics.
  • Collaboration & Support: Support for local designers and tailors through access to quality fabrics and partnership opportunities.

Our Vision

To elevate African textiles on a global scale through innovation, authenticity, and excellence — while empowering creators across the continent.
